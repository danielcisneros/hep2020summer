Welcome to Harvard Ed Portal’s summer internship for high schoolers in Boston! Started in 2020, this program was created to showcase and develop the creative, professional skills of Boston Public School’s students. Before starting the job, students were placed into one of five cohorts tailored to their interests and skills. Over the course of four weeks, the student interns have remotely worked together with other members of their cohort to create a final product. Throughout the process, students have had the opportunity to meet with college advisors and individual mentors, ultimately in preparation for their lives and careers ahead!
